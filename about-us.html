About Us

Overview

At Green Campus, we promote environmental education in over forty universities all around Ireland. We aim to ensure our members can engage in a meaningful way to enhance sustainability on campus. At Green Campus, co-operation is key,we strive for a better future together in hopes that our actions today will help the generations to come.

Our Themes

Litter and Waste, Energy, Water, Transport, Climate Change, Biodiversity and more.

Climate Change

One of the most detrimental environmental issues today is climate change. Global average temperature is expected to warm at least twice as much in the next 100 years as it has during the last 100 years. This is very dangerous and as a generation we need to work together to lower the impact of climate change.

Air Quality

Low air quality can impact our health and our planet. All Universities in the Green Campus programme are vape and smoke free. We encourage to walk,cycle and car-pool where possible.

Procurement

We aim to incorporate sustainability into our procurement process. Our expenses are tracked carefully and thoughtfully to ensure that goods consumed and used on campus are sourced fairly and are environmentally friendly.
