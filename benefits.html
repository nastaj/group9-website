Benefits

We bring many advantages and benefits to facilities under our care

Litter and waste

We are striving to explore practical means for preventing, reducing and minimising the amount of litter and waste produced by the campus.

Energy

We are making an effort to implement means by which the campus community can work together to increase awareness of energy issues and to improve energy conservation and efficiency.

Transport and travel

Our initiative explores ways for students, staff, and local government to work together to raise awareness of transport issues and implement practical solutions to make a real difference to commuter management on campus.

Biodiversity

We examine the diversity of plant and animal life associated with the campus and finds ways to enhance and protect biodiversity.
