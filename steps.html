How it Works

Our initiative is composed of seven simple steps of implementation.

1 2 3 4 5 6 7

01

Setting up the Green-Campus Comitee

The first step is to setup a viable Green Campus committee, incorporating student and staff representatives.

02

Environmental Review

The second step is to carry out an Environmental Review. This is essential before making a plan or creating any goals.

03

Action Plan

The third step is the Action Plan. Once the Environmental Review has been completed, we can evaluate what needs to change and from there can create a prioritised action plan.

04

Monitoring & Evaluation

The fourth step is to Monitor and Evaluate the progress made by the Campus. This evaluation is reported to the Green Campus Committee and from there its decided if any areas need improvement and how much progress was made so far.

05

Linking to Learning on Campus

The fifth step is Learning on Campus. This is important because as a community we want to learn together. Opportunities exist to make learning about Green Campus fun and to make learning links into a range of subject at many levels.

06

Informing and Involving

The sixth step is informing and involving,the point of this step is to spread the message to the community and to the media. The day of action is an opportunity for everyone to get involved and work on a mission in favour of Green Campus.

07

Green Charter

The seventh and last step is a Green Charter. This is a large guidance document outlining Environmental issues.
